# Signals

## Downturn Signals

- ARKK -15% over 3 months
- VIX > 25
- SPY below 200-day moving average

## Recovery Signals

- SPY above 200-day moving average
- QQQ above 100-day moving average
- VIX below 20

## Regime Logic

- Downturn: 2+ downturn signals
- Recovery: 2+ recovery signals
- Otherwise: Mixed
